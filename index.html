Golden Flag Awards

Celebrating the best in Capture the Flag from 2014

The Golden Flag Awards are a new tradition started at the end of 2014 to honor the best in the year's CTF challenges and events. This is a bit of an experiment and based on community feedback, it's possible things will change for next year.

The actual award ceremony will be held Saturday, January 17th at 1800 EST at the ShmooCon conference in Washington, DC, but will be streamed online. Check back here for more information closer to the appointed time.

Current Categories

A large list of nomination categories was shared with the CTF community and based on the feedback from that survey, the most popular categories for awards for final voting were selected. They are:

  • CTF: Most Fun
  • CTF: Most Innovative
  • Challenge: Most Difficult/Complicated
  • Challenge: Most Fun
  • Challegne: Best Crypto
  • Challenge: Best pwnable
  • Most unexpected solution
  • Best tool made for CTF
  • Top CTF Time Team

First, please VOTE. If you have any questions, the best way to talk to us about it is to hop into #pwning on freenode since the various folks involved are usually in there.
